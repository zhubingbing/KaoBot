Established in 1952, Beijing Forestry University is a top-tier public university. It had its first enrollment of international students in 1952 and it was one of the first colleges in China to accept international students. Drawing on its solid foundation in biology and ecology, the University prides itself particularly on its distinctive disciplines such as forestry, landscape architecture, ecology and nature conservation, grass science, and agro-economic management.
